# LinkedIn Content Templates for Recruitin

## Authority Post Templates

### Template 1: Data Insight Post

```
[Surprising statistic about recruitment]

[3 bullet points with specific findings]

De les? [Contrarian or unexpected conclusion]

Wat is jullie ervaring hiermee? [Engagement question]

#recruitment #talentacquisition #hrtech #arbeidsmarkt #recruitin
```

### Template 2: Case Study Post

```

→ [Method description, 2-3 steps]
```

## LinkedIn Newsletter Templates

### Newsletter Structure

```
Subject: [Number + Promise] - e.g., "3 campagne-lessen die je €2.000 per hire besparen"

Opening (100 words):
- Hook with a story or surprising fact
- Promise what the reader will learn

Section 1: [Main insight with data]
- Subheading
- 2-3 paragraphs
- Include one visual/chart reference

Section 2: [Practical application]
- Step-by-step or framework
- Real examples (anonymized)

Section 3: [Common mistakes to avoid]
- 3-5 pitfalls
- Quick fixes for each

Closing (50 words):
- Summary of key takeaway
- CTA: reply, share, or book a call

---
Volgende editie: [Teaser for next newsletter]
```

## Engagement Protocol

After posting, execute this 30-minute engagement window:

1. **First 15 minutes:** Reply to every comment within the first hour
2. **Comment on 5 ICP profiles' recent posts** with meaningful insights (not generic reactions)
3. **Share the post** to relevant LinkedIn groups (max 2 groups per post)
4. **Send to 3-5 warm leads** via DM with personalized context
