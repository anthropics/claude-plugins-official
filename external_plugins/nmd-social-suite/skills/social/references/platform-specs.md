# Platform Specs & Best Practices

## Instagram
- **Caption limit:** 2,200 characters (show 125 before "more")
- **Hashtags:** 30 max — use all 30. Mix mega/mid/niche.
- **Best post times:** 6–9am, 12–2pm, 5–7pm (user's local time)
- **Story slides:** 15 seconds each, text readable in 3 seconds
- **Reels caption:** Keep under 150 words, hook in first 3 words
- **Link:** Only in bio — always reference "link in bio" for blog posts
- **Engagement hook:** End with a question or "save this" CTA
- **Algorithm tip:** First 30 min engagement matters most — reply to every comment fast

## Facebook
- **Post limit:** 63,206 characters (use 200–400 words optimally)
- **Hashtags:** 3–5 max, embedded in text or at end
- **Groups:** Each group has its own audience/rules — always adapt tone
- **Best content:** Personal stories, questions, polls drive most engagement
- **Link previews:** Facebook auto-generates — remove the URL after pasting if you want clean post
- **Best post times:** 1–3pm Tuesday/Wednesday, 12–1pm Saturday

## X / Twitter
- **Tweet limit:** 280 characters
- **Thread:** Use Twitter's native thread feature (+ button)
- **Hashtags:** 1–3 max, ONLY on final tweet
- **Best content:** Hot takes, data, lists, threads with a story arc
- **Thread structure:** Hook → Problem → Insight 1 → Insight 2 → Insight 3 → Takeaway → CTA
- **Best post times:** 8–10am, 12pm, 5–6pm weekdays
- **Engagement:** Retweet your own thread after 2 hours to boost reach

## LinkedIn
- **Post limit:** 3,000 characters
- **Article:** Up to 125,000 characters for long-form
- **Hashtags:** 3–5 at the bottom, no more
- **Structure:** One bold line → blank line → short paragraphs → blank lines between each
- **Best content:** Professional lessons, career stories, industry insights, personal transformations
- **NO:** Salesy posts, humble brags, vague inspiration without substance
- **Best post times:** Tuesday–Thursday, 7–8am, 12pm, 5–6pm
- **First-person voice:** More personal and vulnerable than a corporate brand

## Blog / Website
- **Ideal length:** 1,200–2,000 words for SEO
- **Structure:** H1 title → intro (hook + promise) → H2 sections → H2 takeaways → conclusion + CTA
- **SEO basics:** Primary keyword in title, first 100 words, one H2, and meta description
- **Images:** Include 1 image every 300–400 words (alt text matters for SEO)
- **Internal links:** Link to 2–3 other posts on their site
- **CTA:** End with email signup, follow on social, or comment prompt
- **Meta description:** 155 characters max, include primary keyword, include a benefit
