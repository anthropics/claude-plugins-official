# Hook Formulas — Opening Lines That Stop the Scroll

Use these frameworks to write the first line of every post. The hook is everything.
Never start with "I". Never start with a compliment. Create tension immediately.

---

## The Contrarian Take
"Everyone says [common advice]. They're wrong."
"[Widely accepted thing] is actually [opposite of what people think]."
"Stop [thing everyone does]. It's costing you [specific loss]."

## The Specific Number
"[Specific number] [thing] that changed my [area of life]."
"I made [specific amount/result] in [timeframe]. Here's the exact [strategy/mistake]."
"In [X] months I went from [bad state] to [good state]. This is what actually happened."

## The Uncomfortable Truth
"Nobody talks about [uncomfortable truth in your niche]."
"The reason you're [stuck/failing at X] isn't what you think."
"[Uncomfortable admission] — and it was the best decision I ever made."

## The Question That Hits a Nerve
"Why are you still [thing your audience wastes time on]?"
"When was the last time you [thing they avoid but know they should do]?"
"What would you do if [scenario that forces them to think about their real goal]?"

## The Promise
"[Number] things I wish someone told me about [topic] before I [made the mistake]."
"Everything you need to know about [topic] in [short timeframe/X sentences]."
"The [topic] guide that took me [X years] to figure out."

## The Story Hook
"[Specific time marker], I [did/experienced thing]. [Short dramatic consequence]."
"My [mentor/boss/partner] told me '[quote that changed everything]'. They were right."
"I almost [gave up/quit/failed] at [thing]. Here's what stopped me."

## The Bold Claim
"[Your niche] is broken. Here's how to win anyway."
"Most [people in niche] will never [achieve goal]. Not because they can't — because they [specific reason]."
"[Topic] is the most underrated [thing] in [niche]. And almost no one uses it."

---

## Hook Checklist
Before finalizing any hook, check:
- [ ] Does it create curiosity or tension in one line?
- [ ] Is it specific? (Replace vague with numbers/specifics)
- [ ] Would YOU stop scrolling for this?
- [ ] Does it promise something valuable is coming?
- [ ] Does it NOT start with "I"?
