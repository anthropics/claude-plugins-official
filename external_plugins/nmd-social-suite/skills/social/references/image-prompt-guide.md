# Image Prompt Guide — Visuals That Stop the Scroll

Use these frameworks to generate image prompts for Midjourney, DALL-E, Adobe Firefly, or any AI image tool.

---

## Prompt Structure Formula

```
[Shot type] [Subject description], [Setting/environment], [Lighting], [Mood/emotion], [Color palette], [Style/medium], [Camera details if photo-realistic]
```

---

## Shot Types
- Cinematic wide shot
- Close-up portrait
- Overhead flat lay
- Eye-level lifestyle shot
- Split composition (before/after, comparison)
- Minimal product shot
- Bold typographic card

---

## Style Categories

### Lifestyle / Photo-Realistic (best for Instagram, Facebook)
"Cinematic lifestyle photo of [person/scene], [environment], shot on 35mm film, golden hour lighting, warm color grade, shallow depth of field, authentic and unstaged"

### Bold Quote Card (best for Instagram carousels, Stories, Facebook)
"Minimalist graphic design, bold sans-serif typography centered on solid dark background [color], white text, single accent line in [brand color], clean and modern, no people"

### Motivational/Inspirational Graphic (great for all platforms)
"Dark dramatic background with [texture — concrete / city lights / night sky], bold overlay text in white, cinematic color grade, powerful composition, emotion: [determined/triumphant/focused]"

### Infographic Style (best for LinkedIn, blog, carousels)
"Clean flat design infographic, [number] sections, modern sans-serif font, icons, color palette: [colors], white background, minimal and professional"

### Documentary/Raw (great for authentic storytelling posts)
"Candid documentary-style photo, natural light, slightly grainy texture, authentic real-world scene, no filters, emotion: [real/gritty/honest]"

## Complete Example Prompts

**For a wealth/entrepreneurship post:**
"Cinematic wide-angle photo of a confident young man in a modern penthouse office, floor-to-ceiling city windows, late evening skyline, warm amber desk lamp, dark navy suit, looking out the window in thought. Shot on Sony A7 35mm, f/1.8 shallow depth of field, color grade: deep navy shadows, warm golden highlights. Mood: ambitious, focused."

**For a motivational quote card:**
"Minimalist bold typographic design, dark charcoal background #1a1a1a, white Montserrat Extra Bold headline text: 'STAY LOCKED IN', small gold accent underline, subtle grain texture overlay, centered composition. Clean, modern, no people."

**For a real estate/financial freedom post:**
"Aerial drone shot of luxury suburban homes at golden hour, warm orange sky, long shadows, high contrast, cinematic color grade. Overlaid subtle dark vignette. Mood: aspiration, achievement."

**For a raw storytelling post:**
"Candid black and white photo of a determined young man at a desk late at night, laptop glow, stacks of papers, city lights blurred in background window, grainy film texture, documentary style. Emotion: grinding, relentless."

---

## Quick Tips
- Be specific about skin tone, age range, and setting — vague prompts get generic results
- Mention the exact text for quote cards
- Include negative prompts to avoid: "no watermarks, no blurry faces, no stock photo look, no cheesy poses"
- For Midjourney, add `--ar 4:5` for Instagram feed, `--ar 9:16` for Stories/Reels, `--ar 16:9` for blog/LinkedIn
