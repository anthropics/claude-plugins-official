# Slay the Spire Deck Archetypes

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-25  
**Source:** Community research verified against slaythespire.wiki.gg

This document covers the major deck archetypes for all four characters.

---

## Ironclad Archetypes

### 1. Strength Build

**Strategy:** Accumulate massive Strength, convert to overwhelming damage.

**Core Cards:**

- Demon Form - Gain 2(3) Strength per turn
- Limit Break - Double current Strength
- Inflame - Immediate 2(3) Strength
- Heavy Blade - 3x(5x) Strength multiplier

**Support:** Spot Weakness, Headbutt, Offering, Reaper

**Key Relics:** Vajra, Girya, Energy relics

**Weaknesses:** Slow setup, vulnerable early

**Deck Size:** 15-25 cards

---

### 2. Block/Barricade (Body Slam)

**Strategy:** Retain Block between turns, multiply it, convert to damage.

**Core Cards:**

- Barricade - Block persists
- Entrench - Double Block
- Body Slam - Damage = Block
- Impervious - 30(40) Block burst

**Support:** Feel No Pain, Second Wind, Shrug It Off

**Key Relics:** Calipers (retain 15 Block/turn)

**Weaknesses:** Needs multiple rare picks, inconsistent without thinning

**Deck Size:** 15-25 cards

---

### 3. Exhaust (Corruption)

**Strategy:** Play Skills for free, generate massive Block/draw.

**Core Cards:**

- Corruption - Skills cost 0, Exhaust
- Feel No Pain - Gain 3(4) Block per Exhaust
- Dark Embrace - Draw 1 per Exhaust
- Dead Branch (Relic) - Random card per Exhaust

**Support:** Second Wind, Fiend Fire, Battle Trance

**Weaknesses:** Requires assembling multiple Powers

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

### 4. Self-Damage (Rupture)

**Strategy:** Convert HP loss into Strength, sustain with Reaper.

**Core Cards:**

- Rupture - Gain 1(2) Strength per self-damage
- Brutality - Lose 1 HP/turn, draw 1 card
- Combust - Lose 1 HP/turn, deal 5(7) AoE
- Reaper - Heal based on damage dealt

**Support:** Hemokinesis, Bloodletting, Offering, Feed

**Weaknesses:** HP unsustainable without Reaper

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

## Silent Archetypes

### 1. Poison Build

**Strategy:** Apply poison, multiply with Catalyst for massive scaling.

**Core Cards:**

- Catalyst - Double(Triple) poison
- Noxious Fumes - 2(3) poison/turn to ALL
- Bouncing Flask - Best single-target poison
- Deadly Poison - Direct application
- Corpse Explosion - AoE when enemies die

**Support:** Burst (double Catalyst), Nightmare (3 Catalysts)

**Key Relics:** Snecko Skull, The Specimen

**Win Condition:** Stack poison → Catalyst 2x → 4x → 8x

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

### 2. Shiv Build

**Strategy:** Generate 0-cost Shivs, scale with Accuracy and After Image.

**Core Cards:**

- Accuracy - Shivs deal +4(+6) damage
- Blade Dance - Primary Shiv generator
- After Image - Gain 1 Block per card (0-cost Shivs = free defense)
- Infinite Blades - Shiv each turn

**Support:** Cloak and Dagger, Storm of Steel, A Thousand Cuts

**Key Relics:** Kunai, Shuriken (multi-hit scaling)

**Win Condition:** 10+ Shivs/turn with Accuracy + After Image

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

### 3. Discard Build

**Strategy:** Rapidly cycle deck, trigger discard synergies for energy/draw.

**Core Cards:**

- Calculated Gamble - Discard hand, redraw
- Eviscerate - Cost reduces per discard
- Reflex - When discarded, draw 2(3)
- Tactician - When discarded, gain energy
- Sneaky Strike - 0-cost if discarded

**Support:** Acrobatics, Dagger Throw, Tools of the Trade

**Win Condition:** Infinite-like cycling with Tactician energy + Reflex draws

**Deck Size:** 15-25 cards

---

### 4. Defense/Control Build

**Strategy:** Scale Dexterity, control hand, outlast enemies.

**Core Cards:**

- Footwork - +2(+3) Dexterity
- Well-Laid Plans - Retain 1(2) cards
- Blur - Block carries over
- Backflip - Block + Draw
- Malaise - Weaken boss attacks

**Support:** Leg Sweep, Piercing Wail, Wraith Form

**Win Condition:** High Dexterity → stack Block → damage via poison/shivs

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

## Defect Archetypes

### 1. Focus/Frost (Defensive)

**Strategy:** Build block through Frost orbs enhanced by Focus.

**Core Cards:**

- Defragment - Permanently scale Focus
- Glacier - Channel Frost + Block
- Coolheaded - Channel Frost + draw
- Loop - Channel Frost each turn
- Capacitor - Increase orb slots

**Key Relics:** Inserter (adds orb slot)

**Strengths:** Highly consistent survivability

**Weaknesses:** Slow kills, needs damage solution

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

### 2. Lightning (Offensive)

**Strategy:** Channel Lightning orbs for passive+burst damage.

**Core Cards:**

- Tempest - Channel X Lightning
- Multi-Cast - Evoke orb X times
- Thunder Strike - Dmg scales with Lightning channeled
- Static Discharge - Lightning when hit
- Electrodynamics - Lightning hits ALL
- Defragment - Scale via Focus

**Key Relics:** Ice Cream (save energy)

**Strengths:** High burst, excellent AoE

**Weaknesses:** Can lack defense

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

### 3. Claw (0-Cost Synergy)

**Strategy:** Play many Claws that scale permanently.

**Core Cards:**

- Claw - Gains damage each time played
- All for One - Return all 0-cost from discard
- Hologram - Retrieve from discard
- Scrape - 0-cost attack, draw

**Key Relics:** Shuriken, Kunai, Nunchaku, Pen Nib

**Strengths:** Explosive scaling when assembled

**Weaknesses:** Very inconsistent, needs multiple Claws early

**Deck Size:** 15-25 cards

---

### 4. Creative AI (Power Scaling)

**Strategy:** Generate infinite Powers, amplified by Echo Form.

**Core Cards:**

- Creative AI - Random Power each turn
- Echo Form - First card plays twice
- Biased Cognition, Defragment, Capacitor

**Key Relics:** Mummified Hand (first Power costs 0)

**Strengths:** Extreme scaling

**Weaknesses:** Slow, punished by Awakened One

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

## Watcher Archetypes

### 1. Stance Dance

**Strategy:** Rapidly switch Wrath/Calm to maximize damage while maintaining defense.

**Core Cards:**

- Mental Fortress - 4(6) Block per stance change
- Flurry of Blows - 0-cost, scales infinitely with stance changes
- Tantrum - Recurring multi-hit Wrath entry
- Fear No Evil - Calm entry + damage
- Rushdown - Draw on Wrath entry

**Support:** Empty Fist/Body/Mind, Crescendo, Tranquility

**Key Relics:** Violet Lotus (3 energy from Calm)

**Playstyle:** Constantly change stances for Mental Fortress block + Wrath damage

**Weaknesses:** Vulnerable to Time Eater, needs Mental Fortress

**Deck Size:** 15-25 cards

---

### 2. Retain/Blasphemy

**Strategy:** Accumulate Retain cards, unleash in one Blasphemy turn.

**Core Cards:**

- Blasphemy - Instant Divinity, DIE NEXT TURN
- Windmill Strike - Scales +4(5) damage/turn retained
- Establishment - Retained cards cost 1 less
- Sands of Time - 20 damage Retain
- Meditate - Return from discard, enter Calm

**Support:** Perseverance, Worship (Retain)

**Key Relics:** Runic Pyramid (retain hand)

**Playstyle:** Scale attacks over turns, play Blasphemy when lethal

**Weaknesses:** Slow setup, must kill on Blasphemy turn

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

### 3. Talk to the Hand

**Strategy:** Multi-hit attacks generate massive block.

**Core Cards:**

- Talk to the Hand - Gain 2(3) block per hit on debuffed enemy
- Ragnarok - 5 damage 5(6) times
- Tantrum - Multi-hit, recurring
- Flurry of Blows - 0-cost multi-hit
- Flying Sleeves - 4 damage twice

**Support:** Weave, Consecrate, Wrath/Divinity

**Key Relics:** Kunai, Shuriken, Ice Cream

**Playstyle:** Apply Talk to the Hand → chain multi-hit attacks

**Weaknesses:** Needs Talk to the Hand, less effective vs multiple enemies

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards

---

### 4. Mantra/Divinity

**Strategy:** Generate Mantra to frequently enter Divinity.

**Core Cards:**

- Devotion - 2(3) Mantra/turn (ESSENTIAL)
- Worship - 5 Mantra (Retain when upgraded)
- Brilliance - Damage scales with Mantra
- Prostrate - 0-cost, 2 Mantra + block
- Pray - 3 Mantra

**Support:** Carve Reality, Omniscience, Master Reality

**Key Relics:** Damaru (1 Mantra/turn), Violet Lotus

**Playstyle:** Accumulate Mantra → Divinity every 2-3 turns

**Weaknesses:** Slow setup, needs Devotion

**Deck Size:** 20-30 cards
