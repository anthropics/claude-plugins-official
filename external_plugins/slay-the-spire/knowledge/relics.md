# Slay the Spire: Relics & Events Research Report

## 1. Starter Relics

All data verified from slaythespire.wiki.gg

| Character | Relic             | Exact Effect                                             |
| --------- | ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Ironclad  | Burning Blood     | At the end of combat, heal 6 HP.                         |
| Silent    | Ring of the Snake | At the start of each combat, draw 2 additional cards.    |
| Defect    | Cracked Core      | At the start of each combat, Channel 1 Lightning.        |
| Watcher   | Pure Water        | At the start of each combat, add a Miracle to your hand. |

### Boss Relic Upgrades

Each character's starter relic can be upgraded via boss relics:

- **Ironclad**: Black Blood → Heal 12 HP at end of combat (instead of 6)
- **Silent**: Ring of the Serpent → Draw 2 additional cards at start of combat (same, but replaces starter)
- **Defect**: Frozen Core → Channel 1 Frost if no orbs OR Nuclear Battery → Channel 1 Plasma if no orbs
- **Watcher**: Holy Water → Add 3 Miracles to hand at start of combat (instead of 1)

| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |

### D-Tier Boss Relics

- **Runestone**: Random card transforms (too random)
- **Inserter** (Defect): Channel 1 Dark every 2 turns (weak compared to other options)
